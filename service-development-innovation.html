Partnering for Innovation

ThreadSure's Service Development & Innovation partnerships bring together healthcare providers, technology experts, and service users to co-create solutions that address real-world challenges in recovery care. Through collaborative innovation, we develop evidence-based digital tools that improve outcomes and transform service delivery.

Our Innovation Approach

User-Centered Design

Service users and clinicians guide every aspect of development.

Evidence-Based

All innovations are tested and validated through rigorous research.

Scalable Solutions

Developments that can be implemented across different contexts.

Innovation Partnership Models

Co-Development Partnership

Joint development of new features or services with shared ownership and mutual benefit.

  • • Shared project governance
  • • Joint intellectual property
  • • Co-funded development
  • • Shared risk and reward

Implementation Partnership

Partners help implement and evaluate ThreadSure solutions in their organizations.

  • • Early access to innovations
  • • Implementation support
  • • Evaluation and feedback
  • • Case study development

Research Partnership

Collaborative research to evaluate impact and develop evidence for digital health interventions.

  • • Joint research proposals
  • • Data sharing agreements
  • • Co-authored publications
  • • Research grant applications
