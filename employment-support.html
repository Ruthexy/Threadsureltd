Supporting Career Development in Recovery

Employment is a crucial factor in successful recovery. ThreadSure provides comprehensive employment support services that help individuals develop career skills, find meaningful work, and maintain employment stability. Our person-centered approach recognizes that employment support must be tailored to individual circumstances, skills, and aspirations.

Our Employment Support Philosophy

Individualized Pathways

Career planning based on personal strengths, interests, and recovery goals.

Skills Development

Building practical skills and confidence for workplace success.

Ongoing Support

Continued support to maintain employment and career progression.

Employment Support Services

Career Counseling & Planning

Personalized career guidance to help individuals identify suitable employment pathways and develop career goals.

Includes:

  • • Career interest assessments
  • • Skills and aptitude evaluation
  • • Resume and CV development
  • • Interview preparation
  • • Job search strategies

Skills Training & Development

Practical training programs to develop workplace skills and qualifications for employment success.

Includes:

  • • Vocational training courses
  • • Digital skills workshops
  • • Communication and teamwork skills
  • • Time management training
  • • Certification programs

Workplace Support

On-the-job support and workplace adjustments to ensure successful employment retention.

Includes:

  • • Workplace mentoring
  • • Employer liaison support
  • • Reasonable adjustments advice
  • • Conflict resolution support
  • • Performance coaching

Supported Employment

Structured employment programs with time-unlimited support for individuals needing extra assistance.

Includes:

  • • Job matching and placement
  • • In-work support
  • • Benefits advice
  • • Transport assistance
  • • Ongoing career development
