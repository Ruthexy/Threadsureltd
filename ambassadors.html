Recovery Champions

Our ambassadors are individuals who have experienced recovery journeys and now use their voices to advocate for better mental health and substance misuse care. They bring lived experience to our work, helping us ensure that ThreadSure serves the real needs of those we support.

Their Role

Ambassadors contribute to platform development, share their stories to inspire others, participate in research and evaluation, and help ensure our services remain patient-centered and effective.
