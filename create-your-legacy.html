Ways to Create Your Legacy

Bequest in Your Will

The most common way to create a legacy gift. You can leave a specific amount, a percentage of your estate, or the remainder after other bequests.

Sample Wording:

"I give to ThreadSure Ltd, [amount/percentage/remainder], to be used for its general charitable purposes."

Benefits: No impact on your lifetime finances

Tax Relief: Potentially exempt from inheritance tax

Flexibility: Can be changed at any time

Trusts and Life Interests

Set up a trust that provides income to beneficiaries during their lifetime, with the remainder going to ThreadSure.

Types of Trusts:

  • • Life Interest Trusts
  • • Discretionary Trusts
  • • Charitable Remainder Trusts

Benefits: Provides for family while supporting charity

Tax Relief: Potential inheritance tax advantages

Professional Advice: Consult a solicitor

Pension Scheme Nomination

Nominate ThreadSure as a beneficiary of your pension scheme death benefits. This can provide significant tax advantages.

How It Works:

  • • Contact your pension provider
  • • Complete expression of wish form
  • • No tax on death benefits paid to charity

Benefits: Tax-efficient way to give

Tax Relief: No inheritance tax on pension funds

Impact: Can provide substantial legacy gift

Stocks and Shares

Transfer shares or securities to ThreadSure. This can be particularly tax-efficient if shares have increased in value.

Benefits:

  • • No capital gains tax on transfer
  • • No inheritance tax on gifted shares
  • • Can gift during lifetime or in will

Benefits: Tax-efficient giving

Professional Advice: Consult financial advisor

Flexibility: Can be lifetime or legacy gift
