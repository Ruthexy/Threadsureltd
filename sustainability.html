Environmental Sustainability

Digital Health Benefits

Reduced Travel Emissions

Our platform enables remote consultations and monitoring, reducing the need for patient and clinician travel. This has resulted in an estimated 15,000 fewer car journeys annually across our NHS partners.

Paper Reduction

Digital documentation and communication systems have eliminated thousands of paper forms and reports, significantly reducing paper consumption and associated environmental impact.

Energy Efficient Operations

Our cloud infrastructure uses energy-efficient data centers powered by renewable energy. We continuously optimize our systems to minimize energy consumption.
