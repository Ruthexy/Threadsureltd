Our Organization

ThreadSure Ltd® is a pioneering digital health company specializing in secure, trauma-informed platforms for substance misuse and mental health recovery pathways. Founded in 2020, we emerged from collaboration between NHS clinicians and technology experts who recognized the need for better digital tools in recovery care.

We are registered in England and Wales (Company Number: 12345678) and operate as a Certified Social Enterprise, reinvesting all profits back into improving recovery outcomes and expanding access to our platform.

Key Facts

  • Founded: 2020
  • Headquarters: London, UK
  • Team Size: 45+ specialists
  • Active Users: 10,000+ recovery journeys
  • Certifications: ISO 27001, DTAC, WCAG 2.2 AA
