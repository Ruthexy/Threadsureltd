Make Every Event Count

Fundraising for ThreadSure is a powerful way to support our mission while bringing communities together. Whether you're organizing a small bake sale or a major sponsored event, every pound raised helps us provide life-changing recovery services. We provide all the tools, guidance, and support you need to make your fundraising successful.

Why Fundraise for ThreadSure?

Direct Impact

100% of funds raised go directly to supporting our recovery services.

Community Building

Bring people together for a meaningful cause that benefits everyone.

Full Support
