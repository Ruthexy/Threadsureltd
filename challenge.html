Turn Your Fitness Into Fundraising

Our sponsored challenges combine your love of fitness with the opportunity to make a real difference. Whether you're training for a marathon, cycling to work, or simply walking your dog, you can turn your activity into vital funds for recovery services. Every step, pedal, or stroke brings us closer to supporting more people on their recovery journey.

Why Choose a ThreadSure Challenge?

Flexible Options

Choose from running, cycling, swimming, walking, or create your own challenge.

Community Support

Join a community of like-minded individuals all working towards the same goal.

Track Your Impact

See exactly how your efforts are helping to transform recovery services.
