Specialized Recovery Interventions

Not all recovery journeys follow the same path. ThreadSure's specialist programmes provide targeted interventions for individuals with complex needs, co-occurring conditions, or specific challenges that require specialized approaches. Our evidence-based programmes are designed to address unique circumstances and support successful recovery outcomes.

Specialist Recovery Programmes

Trauma-Informed Care

Specialized support for individuals with trauma histories, using trauma-informed approaches throughout treatment.

Includes:

  • • Trauma assessment and stabilization
  • • EMDR therapy
  • • Somatic experiencing
  • • Safety planning
  • • Trigger management

Dual Diagnosis Treatment

Integrated treatment for individuals with co-occurring mental health and substance use disorders.

Includes:

  • • Integrated assessment
  • • Coordinated treatment planning
  • • Medication management
  • • Symptom management
  • • Relapse prevention

Complex Needs Support

Comprehensive support for individuals with multiple complex needs including homelessness, offending, and mental health.

Includes:

  • • Holistic needs assessment
  • • Multi-agency coordination
  • • Intensive case management
  • • Crisis intervention
  • • Long-term support planning

Women-Specific Programmes

Gender-specific support addressing the unique needs and experiences of women in recovery.

Includes:

  • • Women-only therapy groups
  • • Trauma-informed care
  • • Parenting support
  • • Domestic abuse support
  • • Self-esteem building

Veterans & First Responders

Specialized support for military veterans and emergency service personnel with service-related trauma and addiction.

Includes:

  • • Military cultural competence
  • • PTSD-informed treatment
  • • Peer support networks
  • • Family reintegration support
  • • Transition assistance

LGBTQ+ Affirming Care

Inclusive, affirming support that addresses the unique challenges faced by LGBTQ+ individuals in recovery.

Includes:

  • • LGBTQ+ competent therapists
  • • Identity-affirming care
  • • Community connections
  • • Discrimination support
  • • Family reconciliation
