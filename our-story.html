The Beginning

2020: A Problem Identified

ThreadSure was born from frustration. Our founders, a group of NHS clinicians and technology experts, witnessed firsthand the devastating impact of fragmented recovery care. Patients with substance misuse and mental health challenges were falling through the cracks of disconnected systems, leading to poor outcomes and wasted resources.

"We saw patients cycling through emergency services because their care teams couldn't coordinate effectively. The technology existed, but it wasn't designed for the sensitive, collaborative nature of recovery care." - Dr. Sarah Mitchell, Founding Clinician

Our Journey

2020

Foundation & Research

Initial research phase with NHS partners. Identified key pain points in recovery pathways and began developing trauma-informed design principles.

2021

Prototype Development

Built first working prototype with secure data sharing capabilities. Piloted with 3 NHS trusts, achieving 40% improvement in care coordination.

2022

Expansion & Compliance

Achieved DTAC compliance and GDPR certification. Expanded to 15 NHS trusts and began EU market exploration.

2023

Scale & Impact

Supporting 10,000+ recovery journeys. Launched advanced analytics and AI-driven insights. Recognized with NHS Innovation Award.

2024

Future Focus

Expanding EU presence, developing predictive analytics for early intervention, and leading research in digital health equity.
