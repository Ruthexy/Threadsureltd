Supporting Justice-Involved Individuals

ThreadSure provides specialized support for individuals involved with the criminal justice system, recognizing that many face complex challenges including substance misuse, mental health issues, and social exclusion. Our services work alongside probation services, prisons, and community rehabilitation companies to support successful rehabilitation and reduce reoffending.

Our Justice Support Approach

Addressing criminogenic needs alongside health and social issues.

Risk Reduction

Evidence-based interventions to reduce reoffending risk.

Reintegration Support

Helping individuals rebuild lives in the community.
