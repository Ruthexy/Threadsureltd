Supporting Mental Health & Wellbeing

ThreadSure provides comprehensive mental health support services that address a wide range of psychological needs. Our trauma-informed approach ensures that all services are delivered with sensitivity and respect for individual experiences. We work closely with NHS mental health services to provide integrated care pathways.

Our Mental Health Philosophy

Holistic Approach

Addressing mind, body, and social factors in mental health support.

Person-Centered

Individualized care that respects personal goals and preferences.

Recovery-Focused

Supporting individuals to build resilience and achieve wellbeing.

Mental Health Services

Therapy & Counseling

Evidence-based therapeutic interventions delivered by qualified mental health professionals.

Includes:

  • • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • • Person-Centered Counseling
  • • Solution-Focused Therapy
  • • Mindfulness-Based Therapy
  • • Group Therapy Sessions

Crisis Support

Immediate support for mental health crises with 24/7 access to professional help.

Includes:

  • • Crisis assessment and intervention
  • • Suicide prevention support
  • • Emergency counseling
  • • Safety planning
  • • Follow-up care coordination

Medication Management

Psychiatric medication support and monitoring in partnership with NHS prescribers.

Includes:

  • • Medication reviews
  • • Side effect monitoring
  • • Adherence support
  • • Psychoeducation
  • • Coordination with prescribers

Wellbeing Programs

Preventive and maintenance programs to support ongoing mental health and wellbeing.

Includes:

  • • Stress management workshops
  • • Mindfulness and relaxation
  • • Sleep hygiene programs
  • • Lifestyle and wellness coaching
  • • Peer support networks

Conditions We Support

Anxiety & Depression

Support for generalized anxiety, panic disorder, major depression, and persistent depressive disorder.

Trauma & PTSD

Trauma-informed care for post-traumatic stress disorder and complex trauma presentations.

Personality Disorders

Specialized support for borderline personality disorder and other personality-related difficulties.

Eating Disorders

Support for anorexia, bulimia, binge eating disorder, and other eating-related difficulties.

Bipolar Disorder

Mood stabilization support and psychoeducation for bipolar affective disorder.

Psychosis

Support for schizophrenia, schizoaffective disorder, and other psychotic disorders.
