Our Governance Structure

ThreadSure's Board of Trustees provides independent oversight and strategic direction. Our trustees bring diverse expertise from healthcare, technology, finance, and patient advocacy, ensuring responsible governance and alignment with our mission to improve recovery outcomes.

Our Governance Principles

Independence

Trustees provide independent oversight free from operational conflicts of interest.

Accountability

Regular reporting to stakeholders and transparent decision-making processes.

Expertise

Board members bring diverse skills in healthcare, technology, finance, and governance.

Patient-Centered

All decisions prioritize the needs and dignity of those we serve.
