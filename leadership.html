Lead With Purpose

At ThreadSure, leadership is about more than managing teams – it's about driving innovation, championing recovery-focused care, and making a lasting impact on the lives of individuals and communities. We seek experienced leaders who share our vision of transforming recovery services and are passionate about creating positive change in the health and social care sector.

Innovation-Driven

Embracing new approaches and technologies to improve outcomes.

Values-Based

Leading with integrity, compassion, and commitment to our mission.
