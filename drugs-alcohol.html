Comprehensive Substance Misuse Support

ThreadSure provides evidence-based support for individuals struggling with drug and alcohol misuse. Our integrated approach combines medical treatment, psychological support, and practical assistance to address the complex needs of substance misuse recovery. We work closely with NHS services and community partners to ensure seamless care pathways.

Our Approach

Harm Reduction

Practical strategies to reduce risks associated with substance use while building towards recovery.

Abstinence Support

Comprehensive programs for those seeking complete abstinence from substances.

Relapse Prevention

Skills and strategies to maintain long-term recovery and manage triggers.

Our Services

  • • Medical detoxification

Evidence-Based Treatment Approaches

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Helps identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors associated with substance use.

  • • Thought record keeping
  • • Behavioral experiments
  • • Skills training
  • • Relapse prevention

Motivational Interviewing

Person-centered counseling approach to enhance motivation for change and commitment to recovery.

  • • Exploring ambivalence
  • • Building motivation
  • • Goal setting
  • • Action planning

Contingency Management

Positive reinforcement approach using rewards to encourage abstinence and treatment engagement.

  • • Reward systems
  • • Progress incentives
  • • Treatment adherence
  • • Positive reinforcement
