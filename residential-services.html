Creating Safe Recovery Environments

Stable housing is fundamental to successful recovery. ThreadSure provides a range of residential services designed to support individuals through different stages of their recovery journey. Our residential options combine safety, structure, and therapeutic support to create environments conducive to healing and growth.

Our Residential Philosophy

Safety First

Secure, substance-free environments that prioritize resident wellbeing.

Therapeutic Community

Supportive communities that foster mutual aid and personal growth.

Step-Down Support

Progressive independence with decreasing levels of support over time.
