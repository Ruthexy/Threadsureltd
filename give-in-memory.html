A Meaningful Way to Remember

Losing someone to substance misuse or mental health challenges is devastating. Giving in their memory allows you to honor their life while supporting others on their recovery journey. Your memorial donation creates hope and healing for families and individuals facing similar challenges, ensuring that their legacy lives on through the lives they help save.

How Memorial Donations Help

Healing Through Action

Transform grief into positive action that supports recovery.

Honoring Their Memory

Create a lasting tribute that reflects their compassion and strength.

Supporting Others

Help prevent other families from experiencing similar loss.

Ways to Give in Memory

Individual Memorial Gift

Make a personal donation in memory of a loved one. You can specify how you'd like their memory to be honored.

What We Need:

  • • Name of the person being honored
  • • Your relationship to them
  • • Any special message or tribute
  • • How you'd like their memory honored

Memorial Fund

Create a named memorial fund that can grow over time through contributions from family and friends.

Fund Options:

  • • Named memorial fund
  • • Annual memorial donations
  • • Endowment fund
  • • Scholarship fund

Group Memorial

Organize a group donation from family, friends, or colleagues to honor someone's memory together.

Group Options:

  • • Family memorial gift
  • • Workplace memorial collection
  • • Community memorial event
  • • Sports team memorial

Tribute Gifts

Choose from our range of tribute gifts that can be sent to family and friends as memorials.

Tribute Options:

  • • Memorial cards and certificates
  • • Personalized tribute pages
  • • Memorial trees or gardens
  • • Commemorative items
