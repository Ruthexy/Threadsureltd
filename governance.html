Our Governance Framework

ThreadSure operates under a robust governance framework that ensures responsible, ethical, and transparent management of our digital health platform. Our governance structures prioritize patient safety, data protection, clinical excellence, and organizational accountability.

Governance Principles

Patient-Centered

All governance decisions prioritize the needs and dignity of service users.

Evidence-Based

Decisions are informed by clinical evidence and research.

Transparent

Open communication and clear accountability structures.

Ethical

Adherence to highest ethical standards in healthcare technology.
