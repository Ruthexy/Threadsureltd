Supporting Families in Recovery

Recovery affects the whole family, not just the individual. ThreadSure provides comprehensive family support services that help families understand addiction and mental health challenges, develop healthy communication patterns, and rebuild stronger relationships. Our family-centered approach recognizes that family wellbeing is essential for sustainable recovery.

Our Family Support Philosophy

Family Systems

Understanding how addiction and mental health affect family dynamics and relationships.

Strengths-Based

Building on family strengths and resources to support recovery and resilience.

Inclusive Support

Supporting all family members, including children, partners, and extended family.
