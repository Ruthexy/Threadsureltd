Our Story

Founded in 2020, ThreadSure emerged from a collaboration between NHS clinicians and tech experts frustrated with fragmented recovery systems. We saw the need for a secure, trauma-informed platform that prioritizes patient dignity and data privacy.

Today, we're proud to partner with NHS trusts, justice services, and community rehab teams across the UK and EU, empowering over 10,000 recovery journeys annually.

Key Milestones

  • 2020: Founded by NHS clinicians and tech experts
  • 2021: First NHS trust partnership
  • 2022: DTAC compliance achieved
  • 2023: EU expansion and 10,000+ lives supported
