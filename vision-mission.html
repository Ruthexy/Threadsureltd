Our Mission

"Connecting Care. Empowering Recovery."

To provide secure, trauma-informed digital platforms that connect fragmented healthcare systems, empower patients in their recovery journeys, and enable healthcare providers to deliver more coordinated, effective care for substance misuse and mental health recovery.

Connect

Bridge gaps between healthcare providers, patients, and support networks.

Secure

Maintain the highest standards of data protection and patient privacy.

Empower

Give patients and providers the tools they need for successful recovery.

Our Vision

A world where technology seamlessly supports human-centered recovery care, where data empowers rather than overwhelms, and where every person has access to coordinated, compassionate healthcare that respects their dignity and supports their healing journey.

By 2030, we envision:

  • ✓
    Universal access to secure, trauma-informed digital health tools
  • ✓
    Seamlessly connected healthcare ecosystems that prioritize patient needs
  • ✓
    Data-driven insights that continuously improve recovery outcomes
  • ✓
    Technology that respects human dignity and clinical expertise

Our Core Objectives

Short-term Goals (2023-2025)

Expand NHS Partnerships

Achieve DTAC compliance and integrate with 50+ NHS trusts across the UK.

Enhance Platform Features

Develop advanced analytics and AI-driven insights for better care coordination.

Scale User Base

Support 25,000+ recovery journeys with measurable outcome improvements.

Long-term Goals (2025-2030)

EU Expansion

Establish presence in key European markets with localized compliance and language support.

Research Leadership

Become a global leader in digital health research for substance misuse and mental health recovery.

Industry Standards

Influence development of international standards for trauma-informed digital health platforms.
